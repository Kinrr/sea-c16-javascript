Episode I: A New Hope

Plot

The secret to ending the domination of a brutal intergalactic empire is carried by two robots, sent on a mission to find the last member of a mystical order of knights. Instead, they find a rustic farmboy, Luke Skywalker, who dreams of leaving his monotonous life for adventure. Little did he know he was about to get it...

Along the way, the farmboy finds an elderly hermit, a selfish smuggler and his walking rug, and the princess of a dead planet. Their journey leads them to the rebel alliance, where their bravery and sacrifice allows them to throw off the yoke of the empire....AT LEAST FOR NOW!

Episode II: The Empire Strikes Back

Under Darth Vadar's leadership, the Galactic Empire pursues Luke Skywalker and the rest of the Rebel Alliance. Luke studies the Force with Jedi Master Yoda. When Vader captures Luke's friends, Luke must decide whether to complete his training and become a full Jedi Knight or confront Vader to save his comrades. Also: AT-ATs and Boba Fett.
